About Khwaab

Khwaab was created with a simple belief — that meaningful moments deserve thoughtful, intentional design.

Rooted in Pakistani culture and refined through a modern lens, our work blends tradition with quiet luxury. From weddings and nikkah ceremonies to intimate gatherings and milestone celebrations, we approach every event with care, balance, and attention to detail.

We focus on calm palettes, elegant textures, and cohesive styling — creating spaces that feel warm, elevated, and personal without ever feeling overdone.

Based in the GTA, we work closely with our clients to bring their vision to life while making the process seamless and stress-free.
